nitro cold brew.

Cold brew coffee is brewed with cold water instead of hot. Our cold brew is made by steeping a blend of four premium Arabica beans for 20 hours in our FDA-inspected facility. This process produces a cold brew that is less acidic, has a very smooth texture and subtle sweetness, and contains higher caffeine levels. Customers refer to us as the Bold, Cold, Smooth Cold Brew.
Nitro cold brew is cold brew coffee infused with nitrogen. Nitrogen creates micro-bubbles, giving it a foamy head, sweet flavor, and creamy texture. 4Jacks Nitro Cold Brew contains no sugar or cream, only 9 calories per 12 ounces!
Nitro cold brew is a $2.6b industry growing at a rate of 26% per year. Introducing 4Jacks nitro cold brew to your customers is an easy way to add a substantial revenue source to your F&B business.
We pair our patented nitro dispensers with our distinctive, premium cold brew to create the most convenient, high-quality, and profitable nitro cold brew system in the industry. Our technology involves true nitrogen generation and infusion, unlike many tankless competitors' dispensers. We package our cold brew in recyclable cardboard boxes made of 75% recycled content. Our cold brew is ready to drink, so our dispensers have no water lines, making them versatile for various settings.
