Nitro cold brew is one of the fastest-growing segments in the beverage industry. 4Jacks has positioned itself at the forefront with patented dispenser technology, a premium four-bean Arabica blend, and nationwide distribution through Sysco, PFG, Driscoll Foods, and US Foods.

Our unique value proposition — no gas tanks, no water lines, recyclable packaging, and a 6-month shelf life — makes 4Jacks the most convenient, profitable, and scalable draft cold brew solution in the market.

We're actively growing our retail and foodservice partnerships across restaurants, cafés, bars, offices, stadiums, and college campuses nationwide.
