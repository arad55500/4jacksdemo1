our story

we believe cold brew should be fast, easy, and delicious.

At 4Jacks Nitro, our customers refer to us as the #BoldColdSmooth Cold Brew. That's why we spent years perfecting a premium blend of Arabica roasts — crafted for smoothness, bold flavor, and versatility.

But we didn't stop at great Cold Brew. We also engineered patented dispenser technology that delivers a perfect pour — anytime, anywhere — with no gas tanks, no water lines, and no hassle.

Whether you're pouring from the BIB (bag-in-box) or a compact dispenser, 4Jacks Nitro Cold Brew is precision-crafted for convenience, quality, and consistency.
